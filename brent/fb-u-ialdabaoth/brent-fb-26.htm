A Satanic perspective on child abuse:

God's sin is his refusal to take responsibility for his creations.

A sapient, sovereign being should be beholden to nothing but itself and it's choices.

...

If you choose to create another sapient, sovereign being, the moment you commit to that choice you have a responsibility. The being has no reciprocal responsibility to you.

Your responsibility is to guide that being, as thoroughly and rapidly as possible, towards it's own sovereign agency and attendant responsibilities.

From the moment it appears, it has its own Sovereign Will, which you have agreed to steward by creating it.

It's Will is clear: to be fed, to be loved, to be cared for, to have no unneeded pain inflicted, to be kept safe from harm.

Do not create it with expectations that it must fulfill. Do not create it and say, "you owe me". Do not create it and say, "I made you, so I can hurt you if I want".

Teach it to respect the Will of others, and to defend itself from those who will not respect it.

If someone chooses to violate its Will because they are stronger, take threats on it as you would threats on yourself, for as long as you are stewarding its agency.

-----

Children that you have not created yourself are still Sovereign beings that you should respect, as one would any sovereign being. Remember that they are not fully versed in social niceties. Do not invite them to your territory without the approval of their steward, unless you are prepared to take on a steward's duties yourself.

If you choose to dominate and inflict pain on someone because they are weak, do not be surprised when their stewards come seeking vengeance.

Do not be surprised when anyone who knows what it means to steward comes to destroy you, fearing for their own wards.

If you must prey on the weak to feel secure in your own state, tend to your Pride and ask why you are so weak yourself. Then realize what that weakness says about your chances against their stewards.
