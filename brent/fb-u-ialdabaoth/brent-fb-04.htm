"if the safer people can all take genetic tests to provide evidence that they have affective empathy and the most important human values, they can show each other who they are and the safer people will be able to efficiently cooperate to keep the more exploitative people from causing harm. The safer people have an incentive to do this, and as long as there’s an accurate enough genetic test and all the data is accurate enough, and the genetic testing services are accountable a...nd are hard to cheat, this would give all the safer people a way out.
Caveat: If there is any way to cheat or bribe the genetic testing services, it will happen. What would make it hard to cheat is if everyone *accepted it* if people demand retesting at random for arbitrary reasons, that would help detect bribes and other cheaters. People demanding re-testing should be able to choose any testing organization or sample collection person *they* want. It’s not credible if you let people pick the testing service and people for themselves. The people working at the testing services and also the people collecting samples would have to show that their genes have a safer set of traits that makes them hard to bribe and threaten into lying."
