For some reason, most of the catgirls around here are transgender.

...

So, when a boy notices that he's being treated as weak, and failing, and can't get any kind of positive emotional support for it... and that boy is smart and perceptive, he occasionally decides subconsciously that it'd be better to play the girl-role, because maybe then his life wouldn't be so awfully toxic.

Unfortunately, he tends to ... abstract the girl-role, by focusing primarily on how pretty girls are treated, rather than how average girls - or even unattractive girls - are treated.

And he fails to consider whether becoming a girl will slot him into the 'attractive', 'average', or 'unattractive' bin.

Additionally, he still has a brain that developed with a certain amount of male hormones AND male socialization, so there's a certain kind of low-status male desperation that remains in her behavior post-transition.

This tends to make everything around them a bit awkward.
