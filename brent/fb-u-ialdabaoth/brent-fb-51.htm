Okay, so. What is the Technocracy's "whole deal", in Mage: the Ascension?

Put another way: what is Science? And why do its defenders have to be such assholes, if Awakened Magick actually works?

If some crazy faith healer can *actually cure cancer* by waving crystals around, why would the Progenitors come down on him like a ton of bricks, rather than finding a way to incorporate the trick into the Consensus?

...

The refrain of the Technomancer everywhere is, "yeah, but will the results Replicate?"

The Technocracy are fighting for a particular kind of Ascension, and they're already pretty close to achieving it. In their mind, Ascension is sufficient control of the Consensus that, when a new Rote is created, a mundane version of it is immediately disseminable to the Masses without risk of failure or paradox.

Basically, they want all Sleepers to act as their Acolytes, capable of casting *any* Rote they create.

Crystal-waving is dangerous, not because it doesn't work, but because it won't Replicate. And anyone who puts their faith in crystal-waving is basically interfering with the Replication process, in a dangerous way.

Which basically makes the Verbena your typical anti-vaxxer. They want to erode the Consensus so that their tricks work - *for them* - and don't care that the cost is that a huge swath of the Technocracy's tricks stop working *for everyone*.
