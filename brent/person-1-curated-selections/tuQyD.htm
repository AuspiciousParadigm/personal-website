On 24 / 7 BDSM Relationships and the Treatment of Slaves - Repeat and Clarification

Ok, new version. Much more coherent now.

Prepend: I'm going to use 'he' for the master and 'she' for the slave, here, since I'm a male heterosexual master. Apply whatever pronouns are applicable to your naughty bits and the naughty bits of the people you play with.

In my BDSM experiences, I've found lots of different reasons for slaves and masters to enter a 24/7 agreement. From my perspective, there are four broad categories of slave, based on an active-intellectual-emotional-physical divide - in much the same way that BDSM relationships themselves can divide into [top/bottom] / [dom/sub] / [master/slave]. Each category has different levels of responsibility on the part of the master and on the part of the slave, and mistaking one set of responsibilities for another will invariably lead to frustration. These four levels are:

The Toy (Earth Aspect - Accepting/Unyielding)

The Toy is a touchy concept for most dogmatic 'Safe, Sane and Consentual' types: once she consents to be his, it's the last opportunity to refuse his consent that she'll ever have. Trust me: for this type of slave, it's completely necessary.

The problem with the Toy is, she has no moral compass. She doesn't have the capacity or the desire to understand the consequences of her actions, and all she cares about is her own emotional and physical satisfaction. Never forget that, left to her own devices, HE will become a means to HER ends - not a healthy situation for a Master to be in.

Worse, the Toy is a train wreck. Left to her own devices, she'll destroy the lieves of anyone she can in her quest to get what she wants. She's selfish, petty, thoughtless and malicious, and doesn't care one bit when it doesn't stick to her. She needs to be kept as far away from decent people as possible, which is where you come in.

For the Master, the key to the maintaining your sovereignity with this one is constant and complete domination. Make sure she understands and agrees going into the relationship that '24/7' means she will never be allowed to refuse consent to you again, PERIOD. Do not give her an opportunity to do anything but what you want her to, and do not care how she feels about it. She is an object for you to do with as you please - if she was a person, she'd have shown some capacity to act like one by now. This is ABSOLUTELY vital - if you ever find her starting to develop a real conscience, immediately re-evaluate your relationship with her, and try to nurture it - the "for her own good" schtick only works if you're legitimately serious about it. If you aren't sure if you can skirt SSC like this and still do good, DON'T.

Addendum:
This is important. This is the most important thing about dealing with the Toy; the part that makes her so dangerous. She refuses to believe that's what she is. She will do everything she can to pretend to be the Pupil, the Pet, a bona-fide peer - whatever she thinks will get her the most control, and whatever she thinks she can get away with. If she's clever, she'll gradually start faking emotional and rational responses. They'll be transparently fake if you stay rational about it, but if you let her hook into your emotions (and she'll be VERY good at this), you won't be able to stay rational. You'll start letting her control you, and you'll be ruined. Whenever she starts showing genuine emotional maturity, you HAVE to back off, distance yourself, and study. You HAVE to be rationally certain that she's not faking it to manipulate you. Because if she is, and you believe it, you create just as much damage as if she's genuine and you ignore it. If she's genuine, you MUST stop treating her like a Toy. If she's not genuine, you MUST prevent her from ever trying that tactic on you again. Otherwise, next time she'll get better at it, and eventually she'll win.


The Pet (Water Aspect - Accepting/Yielding)

The Pet is your typical 24/7 slave. She's loving, caring, doting, and doesn't have a thought in her pretty little head besides "how may I serve you, Master?". The Pet is a fully mature human being, but is not a person. Here's the key: she cares deeply about you, she knows right from wrong, she knows that other people's feelings are important, but she either can't or won't think about the consequences of her actions rationally. She follows her heart, not her head, and frequently makes poor rational decisions as a result. This is someone who is dangerous to others, no matter how desperately she wants not to be. As a rational person yourself, you can keep her out of trouble - being your slave is the best thing that could happen to her.

The Pet needs and deserves your love - she's a genuinely caring and appreciative person that just doesn't know any better, and never will. She's a full human being, but not a 'person'. There's a level of responsibility you have towards her, but much simpler than with the Pupil - you're only concerned with her feelings, not her growth or her potential. Feel free to restrict her to what YOU want her to do, as long as her emotional needs are taken care of - feel free to teach her to do what you like, and discourage her from bothering with things you don't care about. This is someone you can keep for the rest of her life, and feel good about.


The Pupil (Air Aspect - Performing/Yielding)

The Pupil is someone who has already demonstrated a desire for rational thought, self-determination, and self-motivation, but is unsure of her motives and her skills. The pupil can be stupid, obstinant, even malicious, but at heart has the desire and the capacity NOT to be. This is a person we're dealing with, and must be respected as such.

The biggest problem I've had with Pupils is that they ARE persons, and therefore deserve my respect. This seems counter to the nature of a 24/7 relationship, until you understand your role in that relationship. With the Pupil, you are just as constrained by their slavery as they are. You have a direct responsibility to train them in ways that are best for THEM, not just in ways that are best for YOU. (Since you have control of the training, of course, it's often possible to achieve both simultaneously.) Likewise, the Pupil may not always be with you - it is very likely that someday, they will grow beyond your capacity to train them, at which point YOU MUST LET THEM GO. Once they're capable of making moral choices as well as you are, it is no longer permissable to keep them as your slave - they must follow their own moral agency from this point forward.

Just because owning the Pupil is a big responsibility doesn't mean it's not fun. Here's someone - a real person - that you get to mold and shape into the sort of person YOU like to be around. Here's someone that is hopefully going to be a good friend for life, even if you let them go - you're showing them all your interests, teaching them to do fun new things with their body, and generally stretching all their mental and physical muscles until they're as good at the kinds of games you like to play as you are. Plus, they're smart and creative, just like you, so once they've learned everything they can learn from you they'll start bringing their OWN ideas to the table. This is a definite win-win.


The Minion (Fire Aspect - Performing/Unyielding)

The Minion is as far as she's going to get mentally at this point in her life, and has voluntarily decided to follow you of her own free will. She is smart enough and capable enough to figure out what her Master would want her to do without direction, and does it without being told or coached. She follows because she knows it's right to follow, because she knows that furthering her Master's goals are the best thing she could possibly do with her life.

For the Minion, often things like punishment and argument and discourse are unnecessary. If she makes a mistake, you can be sure it was either from a simple misunderstanding or a lack of ability, and a quick explanation of the problem should be all that is necessary for her to start immediately working on a solution. The nature of her service is rather hands-off, although the details may be very intimate and responsive. The point is that she doesn't need direction or guidance; she just needs enough information to decide what's best for you and do it. Good luck finding these, but if you do, they're worth their weight in gold.
